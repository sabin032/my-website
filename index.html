About

Background

I grew up in Bhaktapur and completed my Bachelor of Engineering in Electrical Engineering at Khwopa College of Engineering (Top 5 of class, 75.62%). I began teaching almost immediately after graduation — a path that has deepened my understanding of both engineering fundamentals and how to communicate complex ideas clearly. I am a registered engineer with the Nepal Engineering Council (NEC Reg. No. 3032 Electrical 'A') and a member of the Nepal Engineers' Association.
